How Kaarigar works

A simple, honest way to connect people who make things with people who need them.

For customers

  1. 1

    Browse local artisans

    Filter by craft, city, or search for a specific skill.

  2. 2

    View profile & work

    See services, product gallery, prices, and past reviews.

  3. 3

    Request or order

    Send a service request or buy a handmade product directly.

  4. 4

    Track & review

    Manage everything from your dashboard and rate the experience.

For artisans

  1. 1

    Create a free profile

    Add your business name, story, skills, and location.

  2. 2

    List services & products

    Add prices, images, and stock — edit anytime.

  3. 3

    Accept requests

    Get notified when a customer books you. Approve or decline.

  4. 4

    Grow your reputation

    Reviews and repeat customers help your business travel.
