About Kaarigar

The hands that make things
deserve to be seen.

Kaarigar is a marketplace for India's micro-entrepreneurs — the cobbler on your street, the potter throwing terracotta by hand, the tailor who knows exactly how a shoulder should fit. We built this so their skill can travel further than foot traffic ever could.

Direct to maker

No middlemen. Your payment goes straight to the artisan who made your order.

Sustainable & local

Every purchase supports a small workshop and keeps traditional crafts alive.

Community-first

Reviews, ratings, and repeat orders build trust between customers and craftspeople.
